I am a second year Ph.D. student in the [Department of Statistics](http://stat.psu.edu/) at [Penn State University](https://www.psu.edu/), advised by [Dr. Bharath Sriperumbudur](http://personal.psu.edu/bks18/).

Prior to joining Penn State, I worked as a Quantitative Researcher at Goldman Sachs from 2016 to 2017, and as a Model Validation Quant at Nomura from 2015 to 2016. I graduated from the [Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur](https://www.iitk.ac.in/) with an M.Sc.(Integrated) in Mathematics and Statistics under the supervision of [Dr. Debasis Kundu](http://home.iitk.ac.in/~kundu/kundu.html).


My research interests are broadly in the areas of:
----
* *Topological Data Analysis:* Statistical behaviour of Betti numbers, probabilistic properties of persistence modules, topological inference
* *Statistical Learning Theory:* Reproducing kernel Hilbert spaces in probability and statistics, embedding of measures and distributions, empirical processes
* *Deep Learning:* Generative Adversarial Networks, approximation properties, stochastic optimization
* *Stochastic Calculus:* Mathematical finance, Itō calculus for stochastic gradient descent, random processes on manifolds
